Explore the Best Tourist Destinations in India

Uttarakhand Andhra Pradesh Goa Kerala Rajasthan

Uttarakhand

    MUSSORIE
  • Mussoorie: A hill station with stunning views of the Himalayas.
    • JIM CORBETT NATIONAL PARK
  • Jim Corbett National Park: India’s oldest national park, famous for its tiger population.
    • HARIDWAR
  • Haridwar: Known for its ghats and the Ganga Aarti.

Andhra Pradesh

    ARUKA VALLEY
  • Araku Valley: A scenic hill station, known for its coffee plantations.
    • CHILIKA LAKE
  • Chilika Lake: A large coastal lagoon, popular for bird watching.
    • TIRUPATI
  • Tirupati: Famous for the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple.

Goa

    CALANGUTE Beach
  • Calangute Beach: Known as the "Queen of Beaches," it's a popular spot for tourists.
    • OLD GOA
  • Old Goa: Famous for its colonial architecture and churches.
    • DUDHSAGAR
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls: A beautiful four-tiered waterfall amidst the Western Ghats.

Kerala

    ALLEPPEY
  • Alleppey: Known for its backwaters and houseboat cruises.
    • MUNNAR
  • Munnar: A scenic hill station, famous for tea plantations.
    • WAYANAD
  • Wayanad: Known for its wildlife sanctuaries and waterfalls.

Rajasthan

    JAIPUR
  • Jaipur: Known as the "Pink City," it’s famous for palaces and forts.
    • UDAIPUR
  • Udaipur: Known for its lakes and the City Palace.
    • JAISALMER
  • Jaisalmer: Famous for its golden forts and desert landscape.
